Places to Visit

From ancient ruins to natural wonders

Must-See

Door to Hell (Darvaza Crater)

Darvaza, Ahal Province

A natural gas crater burning since 1971. One of the most surreal sights in the world — best visited at night when the flames glow against the darkness.

Mary Province

Ancient Merv

A UNESCO World Heritage Site — once one of the greatest cities of the ancient world and a key stop on the Silk Road.

Capital City

Ashgabat

The world's whitest city — known for its stunning white marble architecture, golden statues, and wide boulevards.

Balkan Province

Yangykala Canyon

A breathtaking canyon of red and pink sandstone cliffs near the Caspian Sea. One of Turkmenistan's most dramatic natural landscapes.

Near Ashgabat

Nisa Fortress

Ruins of an ancient Parthian capital dating back to the 3rd century BC. A UNESCO-listed site of immense historical significance.
